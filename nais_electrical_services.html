Services

NAIS provides multi-discipline support to help clients protect asset integrity, improve reliability, and deliver critical scopes safely — onshore and offshore. From inspection and maintenance through to specialist rigging, mechanical and electrical support, we work as a dependable partner from planning through to completion.

Electrical Fabric Maintenance Inspection & Integrity Renewables Rigging, Mechanical & Construction

Electrical

NAIS has a proven track record in the application of integrated electrical solutions, working closely with customers from project conception through to commissioning and completion. We focus on safe delivery, clear documentation, and a reliable handover.

Typical support includes:

  • Electrical installation support
  • Testing and commissioning assistance
  • Coordination with multidisciplinary works on live sites and shutdowns

Fabric Maintenance

A critical part of maintaining asset integrity and increasing lifespan, NAIS provides the full array of fabric maintenance services — from survey/inspection through to preparation and coating application. We deliver demanding maintenance scopes across a wide variety of environments and structures.

Typical scopes include:

  • Paint inspection & survey
  • Surface preparation
  • Coatings application
  • Blasting / spraying
  • Insulation works

Inspection & Integrity

We provide a comprehensive range of specialist integrity and inspection services to the oil & gas, petrochemical, renewable and industrial sectors. Our inspection support helps clients identify issues early, plan maintenance effectively, and reduce operational risk.

Typical scopes include:

  • NDT inspection support
  • Condition surveys and reporting
  • Inspection input for repair planning and close-out

Renewables

NAIS has built substantial knowledge and expertise supporting offshore wind, including experience across multiple offshore windfarm fields. Our teams work on assets ranging from monopile foundations and jacket structures to offshore substations, delivering practical maintenance and repair support in marine environments.

Typical scopes include:

  • NDT inspection
  • Repairs & composite wraps
  • Marine growth removal
  • Coating repairs
  • Mechanical repairs

Rigging, Mechanical & Construction Services

Our highly experienced OPITO/NVQ and trade qualified riggers are capable of delivering complex rigging and lifting solutions both on and offshore. We also support a wide range of mechanical and construction workscopes, including shutdown-critical activities.

Typical scopes include:

  • Caisson removal / replacement
  • Structural installation
  • Decommissioning works
  • Pipefitting
  • Assisting with shutdown-critical scopes
